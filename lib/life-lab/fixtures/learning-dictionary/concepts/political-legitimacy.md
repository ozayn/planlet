# Political legitimacy

**Definition**

The belief that a government or ruler is rightful and entitled to obedience, not merely feared or tolerated.

## Dictionary candidates

- Succession ritual
- Public memory

## Optional Flashcards

Q: What is political legitimacy?
A: The belief that rule is rightful, not merely enforced.

Q: Why does legitimacy matter during succession?
A: It shows whether institutions still command moral authority after symbolic loss.
